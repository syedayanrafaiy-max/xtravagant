About EXTRAVAGANTÈ

EXTRAVAGANTÈ is a self-made brand born out of a passion for timeless, old-money style fashion. Based in Karachi, Pakistan, we specialize in creating garments that exude class and sophistication while remaining accessible and budget-friendly.

Our products are crafted with precision, using only high-quality materials, ensuring that you not only look your best but feel comfortable in every piece. We believe in quality over quantity, and every item is designed to be a lasting addition to your wardrobe.

Currently, we only ship within Karachi at a shipping rate of 150 PKR. Customer satisfaction is our priority, and we aim to provide an exceptional shopping experience for all our clients.
