About EXTRAVAGANTÈ

EXTRAVAGANTÈ is more than a clothing label — it is a statement of refined taste, timeless style, and uncompromising quality. Born in the heart of Karachi, Pakistan, our brand is the work of a self-made vision, blending the elegance of classic old-money fashion with the accessibility of modern craftsmanship.

We source the finest fabrics and focus on meticulous tailoring, ensuring every piece delivers both comfort and sophistication. Our collections are designed to be worn with pride, to tell a story of elegance in every stitch.
